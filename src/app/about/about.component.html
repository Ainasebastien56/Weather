About Our Weather Application

Welcome to our Weather Application, your go-to source for accurate and up-to-date Weather information. Whether you're planning your day, preparing for a trip, or simply curious about the weather conditions in different parts of the world, our app has you covered.

Key Features:

Current and Future Weather Forecasts:

Get detailed current conditions and upcoming weather forecast.

Interactive Map:

Visualize weather patterns globally and search for specific locations.

Weather News:

Stay updated with the latest weather-related news and alerts.

Technologies Used

Angular: A powerful framework for building dynamic web applications.

Tailwind CSS: A utility-first CSS framework used to create a modern, responsive design.

WeatherAPI: Provides real-time and forecast weather data from around the world.

NewsAPI: Delivers up-todate news articles related to weather events.
